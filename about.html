Studio Philosophy

Our philosophy of care focuses on architecture that is functional, economical, structurally sound, and aesthetically appealing.

We are based in Westlands, Nairobi and collaborate closely with clients to ensure every project is delivered with clarity and excellence.

Our Story

After graduating from campus, Braxton Muma began taking on solo architectural design projects. That early independent work became a major source of referrals and steadily expanded his client base. At the time, he operated from his father's home, which served as his first office.

As demand grew, Braxton decided to formalize the practice into a company and called his close campus friend, Ibrahim Koros, whose consistency and work ethic he deeply trusted. While they were developing the company name, they also identified the need for strong structural expertise and brought in Wilfred Omondi, a structural specialist with more than seven years of design experience and a proven track record from their campus years. Together, they agreed on the name Tri-EcoTeq Architects.

Later, they onboarded Joseph Mbugua, a former campus colleague recognized for exceptional 3D software modelling and high-quality design rendering.
